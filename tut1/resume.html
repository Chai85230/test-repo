Electric power generation

Description:

Electric power generation of footstep using rack and pinion method and piezoelectric sensors. In this project,we're using an unconventional method simply running or walking on a Footstep to generate electricity. Whileusing the Rack and Pinion method, mechanical energy is transformed into electrical energy using a dynamometer. Piezoelectric sensors will convert mechanical strain into voltage. In this project we are combining both the methods, Rack and Pinion and Piezoelectric sensors, to generate electricity.
